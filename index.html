About Me

Hello, my name is Bill Bittner. I enjoy lots of things, but mostly playing music, making art, being in nature, woodworking, and playing sports. I also really like computers and programming. I am fascinated by the web and how it continues to grow, and I am excited to make some cool websites for it!

I grew up in Sacramento, California, but I moved to Los Angeles to go to USC and I have lived here ever since. It takes some getting used to, but now that I have learned to love it, it is one of my favorite cities in the world.
